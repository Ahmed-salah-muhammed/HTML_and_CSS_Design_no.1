Project 1

The project is to manage the Risks Assessment of the environment of Salloum City.

story map for this project

-We have prepared environmental studies that lead us reach the Risk Assessment :- 1- Ground studies (contour - topography - slopes - soil - geology ...) 2- Water studies (floods - watercourses ...) -Hence the most dangerous Hazard was identified and it was Torrents and we make potential scenarios. -A vulnerability map was made for the damaged buildings and the population and families at risk . Then it was done :- 1- An Immediate Action plan. 2- A short-term plan. 3- A long-term plan. -And the reflection of studies on the plan for Salloum.

Project 2

Here you can see our project to study the urban atmospheric environment of urban design alternatives for a residential group in El Gouna city in the Red Sea. The focus was on thermal comfort indicators and comparing them to various alternatives to determine the optimal one among them. Envi-met was used to perform the simulation, and ArcGIS pro to visualize and compare the simulation results

Project 3

this project about environmental studies

story map for this project

preparations for the eastern Assiut region to study the possibility of its developmental exploitation in the light of dealing with natural hazards, precisely the danger of floods, and treating it with planning methods.
